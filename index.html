Hello there!

From the 1st September 2019 I will be teaching a yoga class in the Featherbrooke Lapa. Whether you’re a well-practiced yogi or a complete newbie, the class will be cater for all levels of ability.
The class is open to all ages, fitness levels, shoes sizes, guys and girls... Please don’t think that if you can’t touch your toes then you can’t do yoga!
Please bring your own mat, and come dressed in comfortable clothing, something that will keep you warm but allow you to stretch as well. A small hand-towel and a water bottle would be a good idea too!
